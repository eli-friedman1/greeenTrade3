FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Will GreenTrade buy my broken phone?

Yes. We are so committed to the environment that we want to dispose of your broken phone properly and will pay you to give us the honor to do so. Such an honor is not paid for at the same price of a functioning cellphone. The condition of the phone is a large factor in the price we will pay for the phone but we will even buy broken phones.

How do I schedule a GreenTrade Pick-Up?

Once you are in a GreenTrade certified pick-up zone we will give you a date selector and a time pick-up option for when a certified GreenTrade Pick-Up driver will get your phone from you and deliver it to us. We are awesomely convenient! We are awesomely good at picking up your phone from you and we are awesomely good at paying you for it.

How do I get paid?

Once we get your phone, we have a 30 point inspection to make sure the phone matches the condition selected. Once we confirm the phone is in good standing we will issue you a payment based on your selected preferred method. PayPal. Check, money order or gift cards..

When do I get paid?

Once GreenTrade receives the phone and ensures that the phone is in the condition that was input online, GreenTrade will issue you a payment. You will receive an email notification with the payment as well to make sure that you are notified that you received money.

What is "Green" about GreenTrade?

Aside from the money you will be making, GreenTrade endeavors to dispose and recycle phones in a way that is healthy for the planet. You would be surprised as to how many phones land up in land-fills that have a negative impact and increase the carbon footprint. Green Trade ensures that every phone is recycled and disposed of properly.

How do I become a GreenTrade driver?

If you are interested in our mission and would like to be a beacon of hope for our environment please contact us directly at info@greentrade.co to get started on the application to becoming a GreenTrader.

Will GreenTrade pick-up if I am not at home?

Once a GreenTrade driver is nearby you will feel it in the air. We wish but in case its foggy we will text you when our driver is nearing the pick-up location at the selected time. If your day is not going as planned and you are stuck in a checkout jam at your favorite organic market or it's the season finale of your favorite show, we will re-schedule for another time that suits you. You are always at the front of the line with GreenTrade.
